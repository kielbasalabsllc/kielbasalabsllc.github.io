Fade to Bright

Mac

Go beyond max brightness.

Fade to Bright unlocks your Mac display's full brightness potential. It uses Apple's built-in support for higher brightness to push your screen past the standard ceiling, so you can see more, see better, and work comfortably even in bright environments.

Boost the full screen or pick a specific section. No background processes, no fuss. Just a brighter display, exactly where you need it.

Features

  • Go brighter than the default settings using Apple's built-in display capabilities
  • Boost the full screen or a selected area
  • Real-time nits readout for your display
  • Lightweight menu bar app, always within reach
