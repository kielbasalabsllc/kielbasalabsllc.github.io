Privacy Policy

Kielbasa Labs Geo Bookmarks

Overview

Geo Bookmarks is built around the principle that your data is yours. All bookmarks, notes, and photos are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to Kielbasa Labs or any third party.

Location Data

Geo Bookmarks accesses your device's location to save and display bookmark coordinates and to calculate your distance from saved places. Location is accessed only when you actively use the app. Your location data is stored on your device and is never sent to any external server.

Photos

Photos you attach to bookmarks are stored locally on your device. Geo Bookmarks may request access to your photo library so you can select existing photos. No photos are uploaded or shared.

Data We Do Not Collect

Geo Bookmarks does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or any identifying information. There are no accounts, no servers, and no backend of any kind receiving data from this app.

Third-Party Services

Geo Bookmarks uses Apple Maps to display map views and to open saved locations. Apple's own privacy policy governs any data handled by Apple Maps. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

Geo Bookmarks is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

Contact
