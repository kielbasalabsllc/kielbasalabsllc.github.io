Privacy Policy

Kielbasa Labs BrightenUp

Overview

BrightenUp is a local-only app. It interacts only with your Mac's display hardware and stores no personal data. There are no accounts, no sync services, and no servers.

Display Data

BrightenUp reads display capabilities (such as peak nits and EDR support) solely to control brightness on your device. This information is never transmitted to Kielbasa Labs or any third party.

Data We Do Not Collect

BrightenUp does not collect analytics, usage statistics, crash reports, or any identifying information. We have no visibility into how you use the app or what settings you choose.

Third-Party Services

BrightenUp does not integrate with any third-party services. No external SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks are included in the app.

Children's Privacy

BrightenUp is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

Contact
