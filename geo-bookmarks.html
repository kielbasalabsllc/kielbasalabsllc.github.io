Geo Bookmarks lets you save any place that matters to you — with photos, notes, and everything you want to remember about it.

See how far you are from each saved spot. Open any bookmark in Maps in one tap. Built for travelers, regulars, and anyone who finds a place they want to come back to.

Features

  • Save locations with custom names and notes
  • Attach photos to remember the details
  • See your distance from each bookmark
  • Open any saved place directly in Maps
  • For iPhone and iPad
